Idea for the game

Minimum viable product
- First-person platformer
- Enemies walk backwards and forwards on platforms
	- Player dies if they touch an enemy
- One level
	- Player tries to complete level as quickly as possible
	- Score (time) is displayed on screen
- Play sounds
	- Happy sound when you get to the end
	- Sad sound when you die
- When you get to the end of the level, show a screen with the final score/time
- Menu items
	- Slider selects skin of player
	- Toggle 


Extra things
- Player can sprint
- Jump pad
- Speed boost
